Instructions and Credits

Welcome to the Power Rangers tabletop RPG dice roller.

To start using the dice roller, open the character sheet and enter your name and your skill die level for each skill.

Ready to roll? Select the skill you want to roll from the dropdown menu along with any applicable modifiers and click the roll button. Your results will appear in the roll results box.

Do you need to roll a custom pool of dice? Click the dice bag button to open the dice bag to roll a custom mix of dice.
